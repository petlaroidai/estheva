Henna is a form of body art from Ancient India that is applied on hands, feet, or other body parts. For those who don't know anything about henna tattooing, it's an ancient practice used to create designs with significant and often spiritual meaning. It's generally used today to express good luck, happiness, and is often featured at ceremonial events like weddings, graduations, maternities, and birthdays. Henna tends to last approximately two weeks.
